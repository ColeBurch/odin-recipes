Chicken Pot Pie

Description

This chicken pot pie recipe is my wife and I's favorite meal to make when we bring dinner to our friend's homes.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of butter
  • 1 onion
  • 4 carrots
  • 4 celery stalks
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1/3 cup of flour
  • 1 3/4 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 pre-made pie crusts
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees farenheit
  2. Cook chiken breast in whichever method you choose then shred. My wife and I usually use a pressure cooker to quickly cook the chicken breast from frozen.
  3. Melt butter in pan and add all vegetables except for corn. Cook until vegetable are soft.
  4. In seperate container, mix milk and chicken broth.
  5. Add flour to pan and mix thoroughly.
  6. Slowly add the milk and chicken broth mixture to the pan in increments. Mix while adding and do not add next increment until the mixture is thick.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Remove from heat and mix in shredded chicken and corn.
  9. In a pie dish, lay the first pie crust into the dish, making sure to cover the entire dish including the sides.
  10. Pour in the filling mixture and cover with the second pie crust.
  11. Working your way around the perimeter of the pan, push the edges of the two pie crusts together and fold into the center.
  12. Cut a venting pattern into the top pie crust.
  13. Bake covered for 40 mins. Remove foil and bake an additional 5 mins.
  14. Enjoy!
