Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a lasagna recipe that my wife and I love to make. It has lots of cheese and is great any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 eggs
  • 15oz of ricotta cheese
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 48oz of Rao's red sauce
  • 3 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cups of parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Chop onion and cook together with beef in a pan.
  3. Once beef is cooked, remove from heat and add chopped garlic and Rao's red sauce to the pan, stir until well mixed.
  4. In a seperate bowl, mix the eggs, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. Add salt, pepper, and Oregano to taste.
  5. In cooking dish, layer the ingredients in the following order until the dish is full.
    1. Sauce mix
    2. Noodles
    3. Cheese mix
  6. Once pan is full, top with mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cook covered for 50 mins. Remove foil and cook an additional 10 mins.
  8. Enjoy!
