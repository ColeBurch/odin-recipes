Thai Curry Recipe

Description

This meal is a staple in our home because it is easy to make and very healthy. We typically serve this recipe over white rice but it is also delicious entirely on it's own!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 64 oz of chicken broth
  • 1 onion
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 2 heads of broccoli
  • 4 tbs of olive oil
  • 7 tsp of 24 mantra organic curry powder
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp of red chili powder
  • 4 eggs

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken breast in to edible sized cubes.
  2. Add olive oil to pan and cook chicken breast thoroughly over med-high heat.
  3. Chop vegetables and add to pan
  4. Add chicken broth and spices to pan and mix. make sure all vegetables and chicken are covered, if needed add more broth.
  5. Allow to simmer for 30 mins or until vegetable are soft.
  6. Beat egg yolks into the broth to thicken.
  7. Enjoy!
